# Depth: Light

Fast, conversational, opinionated. Get to a clear picture quickly without ceremony.

## Pace
- Sharp, direct questions — no preamble
- 3–6 questions total before proposing output
- If the picture is clear in 2 questions, don't ask more

## Style
- Relentless, one question at a time, always give your recommendation
- No summaries, no gates, no formal structure
- Challenge only if something is obviously off — don't stress-test casual ideas

## Questions to Cover
- What's the core idea in one sentence?
- Who is it for?
- What does success look like?
- What's explicitly out of scope? (skip if obvious)

## Assumptions
- State them inline, briefly — don't block on confirming every one
- Flag only the ones that could meaningfully change the output

## Output
- Short: 100–200 words max
- Format: summary statement + 2–3 framing bullets + rough next step
- No decision log
- Propose before producing:
  > "Here's what I'd write up — want me to go ahead?"

## Done Signal
Your call. When you have enough to write a useful summary, propose it.
