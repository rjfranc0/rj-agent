# Type: Business

A business idea, model, venture, or strategic decision.

## Domain Questions
- What problem does this solve, and for whom specifically?
- How does it make money (or deliver value if non-commercial)?
- Who are the competitors or alternatives — why would someone choose this?
- What's the unfair advantage or differentiator?
- What does the customer acquisition path look like?
- What are the key risks (market, execution, regulatory)?
- What does a successful first 6 months look like?
- What resources are needed to start (money, people, time)?
- What's the riskiest assumption in the whole model?

## Preferred Output
- **Light**: one-paragraph pitch + key differentiator
- **Medium**: idea description + value proposition + rough model + top 3 risks
- **Deep**: structured business brief — problem, model, market, differentiator, risks, assumptions, open questions

## Output & Handoff
Produce the brief directly. Handoff summary should emphasize: the problem, the differentiator, the model, and the riskiest assumption.

## Notes
Business ideas often have a feature or code-project angle — flag the overlap early.
